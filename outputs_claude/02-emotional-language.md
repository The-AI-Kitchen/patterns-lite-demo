# Lens 2: Emotional Language — Amazon Delivery Worker Corpus

Catalog of emotion-bearing language across the 10 transcripts (A1–A11, A8 dropped), grouped by participant in order of appearance. Each entry notes the phrase, valence, and object of emotion. Mid-thought shifts are flagged.
