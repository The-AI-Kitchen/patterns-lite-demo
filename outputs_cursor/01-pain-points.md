# Lens 1: Pain Points

Analysis of five interview transcripts on how SCU students track academic deadlines. Pain points are grouped by transcript in order of appearance and tagged by severity (Blocker / Major / Minor). Each entry includes a verbatim citation.

---

  - Severity: Major
  - Quote: "not every professor uses it the same way."

  - Quote: "I spent like two hours setting up this beautiful database and then I never opened it again."
  - [p01: "I spent like two hours setting up this beautiful"]
4. Internal group deadlines live in Slack and must be manually re-entered into Reminders.
  - Severity: Major
  - Quote: "if I waited for him to actually do it I'd never see his contribution."
  - [p01: "if I waited for him to actually do it"]
5. Camino email notifications were missed because they arrived overnight during Do Not Disturb.
  - Severity: Blocker (resulted in missing a post)
  - Quote: "I missed a Camino post because the email came in at like 11pm and I had Do Not Disturb on."
  - [p01: "I missed a Camino post because the email came"]
6. Email notifications are not trusted, forcing a manual morning check of Camino.
  - Severity: Major
  - Quote: "I don't trust email notifications."
  - [p01: "I don't trust email notifications."]
7. The daily morning sweep across Camino, Slack, and email takes 15+ minutes.
  - Severity: Major
  - Quote: "Like fifteen minutes? Sometimes longer."
  - [p01: "Like fifteen minutes? Sometimes longer."]
8. The Camino iCal feed produced duplicate events that could not be resolved.
  - Severity: Blocker (caused abandonment of the integration)
  - Quote: "it kept showing duplicate events and I couldn't figure out which one to delete."
  - [p01: "it kept showing duplicate events and I couldn't figure"]
9. Running Google Calendar alongside Reminders produced conflicting entries.
  - Severity: Major
  - Quote: "they'd disagree, and now I'm checking three things instead of two."
  - [p01: "they'd disagree, and now I'm checking three things instead"]
10. Slack group-chat deadlines scroll out of view and must be manually translated to Reminders.
  - Severity: Major
    - Quote: "those deadlines live in Slack, in our group chat, which scrolls."
    - [p01: "those deadlines live in Slack, in our group chat,"]

  - [p04: "I open Camino to find the assignments, but I"]

  - Severity: Minor
  - Quote: "If the sticky gets too messy I rewrite it on a fresh one."
  - [p04: "If the sticky gets too messy I rewrite it"]

  - Severity: Major (abandoned the tool)
