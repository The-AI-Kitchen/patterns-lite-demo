## Hypotheses for further inquiry

1. **Hypothesis:** When notifications feel mistimed or non-actionable, students **replace pushes with fixed cadence LMS checks** instead of tuning in-product notification settings. **Evidence:** [p04: "They came at midnight, they came on Sunday morning, they didn't respect any kind of human rhythm."] (pain), [p03: "The notifications are a joke."] (emotion), [p07: "Off. I check Camino on Sunday. The notifications were not"] (chain). **Counter-evidence:** Peers who keep LMS notifications on and still avoid weekly intake rituals.

2. **Hypothesis:** **Notion** fails when it forces **meta-design before execution**, producing shame or anxiety that one-line list UIs avoid. **Evidence:** [p01: "I tried Notion last quarter and it was,"], [p04: "I made a whole reading log and then I wrote in it three times. It's a graveyard."], [p10: "I tried Notion. The blinking cursor and the empty page made"]. **Counter-evidence:** Sustained Notion use without negative affect in the same workload conditions.

3. **Hypothesis:** **Dual recording** normalizes reconciliation pain instead of ending it. **Evidence:** [p01: "I tried using my Google Calendar more, but then I'd put stuff in Google Calendar AND Reminders, and they'd disagree, and now I'm checking three things instead of two."], [p06: "The bigger annoyance is when Trello and the spreadsheet disagree about when something's due, and I have to figure out which one is wrong."], [p06: "Sometimes I do double entry, like a Trello deadline that also affects my personal week, but I just live with it."] **Counter-evidence:** Participants who collapse to one system of record after the first conflict.

4. **Hypothesis:** **Professor-specific delivery** increases **per-class hunting**, feeding weekly manual pulls. **Evidence:** [p01: "Like in 174 the prof posts everything on the course website, not in Camino."], [p09: "So I have to open the syllabus, scroll to the right week, find the assignment, then go back to Modules to actually submit. Every assignment."], [p08: "I pull deadlines into GitHub Projects manually every Sunday night."] **Counter-evidence:** Uniform LMS posting correlated with shorter intake rituals for the same students.
