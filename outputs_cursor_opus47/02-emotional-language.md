# Lens 2: Emotional Language

This catalog stays strictly within the emotional-language lens. Each entry reports a participant's emotion-bearing phrase, its valence, the object the emotion attaches to, and (where applicable) a note on intensity shifting mid-sentence or mid-paragraph. Pain-point friction, causal chains, and solution moves are out of scope and excluded.

Participants are grouped by transcript and ordered by appearance. A8 is intentionally absent from the corpus.
