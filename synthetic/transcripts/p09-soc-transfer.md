Topic: How students track academic deadlines at SCU

I: How are you keeping track of assignments this quarter?

P9: Honestly, badly. I transferred from De Anza in September and Camino is, I'm still figuring it out. At De Anza we used Canvas which I guess is the same thing but different? It looks different. I don't know where the assignments are half the time.

I: Tell me about a specific class.

P9: My SOCI 169 prof posts assignments in a tab called "Modules" but the due dates aren't on the assignments themselves, they're in the syllabus. The syllabus is a PDF. So I have to open the syllabus, scroll to the right week, find the assignment, then go back to Modules to actually submit. Every assignment.

I: How does that feel?

P9: I feel dumb. I asked my friend in the same class how she does it and she said she just remembers. I don't believe her.

I: What are you using right now to track?

P9: Email. I email myself every Sunday with a list of what I think is due that week. It's not a great system. Last week I had three things due that I didn't have on my Sunday list. I missed one.

I: Have you tried any apps?

P9: I downloaded Notion because everyone says Notion. I opened it once. It's a wall of empty pages and I didn't know where to start. I closed it.

I: What about Google Calendar?

P9: I use it for work shifts and that's it. I haven't put school in there. Maybe I should.

I: What would make this easier?

P9: I want someone to just show me how it's supposed to work here. Like, a quarter-long onboarding for transfer students. The first few weeks I was just guessing. I'm still mostly guessing.

I: Group projects?

P9: I have one in SOCI 169. We use GroupMe. The deadline for our group paper is in the syllabus but our internal milestones are wherever someone in GroupMe last typed them. I scroll back and try to find them.

I: How do you feel about all this?

P9: Tired. Behind. A little embarrassed. I was a 4.0 student at De Anza and here I feel like I'm always one assignment behind.

I: One change?

P9: Make the due date show up on the assignment, not just the syllabus. That alone would help me so much.
