# Transcript p05 — Synthetic / Example Data

EXAMPLE DATA: Fictional interview. No real participant is represented.

Participant: P5, Math sophomore, three classes (Real Analysis, Linear Algebra, Discrete Math) plus a tutoring job
Topic: How students track academic deadlines at SCU

---

I: How do you keep track of what's due?

P5: I just remember. Honestly. I know that sounds dumb.

I: Walk me through that.

P5: My three classes have problem sets due on the same day every week. Real Analysis is Wednesday, Linear is Friday, Discrete is also Friday. So Wednesday morning I do Real, Thursday I do Linear, Thursday night or Friday morning I do Discrete. It's the same pattern every week. There's nothing to track.

I: What about exams or one-off assignments?

P5: Exams are on the syllabus. I look at the syllabus at the start of the quarter and I know roughly when they are. If a prof adds something unusual, I'll write it on the back of my hand. Like literally on my hand. Sounds insane but it works.

I: Camino?

P5: I check Camino maybe twice a week. Mostly to download problem sets when they drop. I'm not in Camino to manage my life. I'm in Camino to download a PDF.

I: Group projects?

P5: I don't really have group projects in math. We have a once-a-quarter proof presentation in pairs. The deadline is on the syllabus. We text each other to coordinate.

I: How does this feel? Are you ever anxious?

P5: No, mostly not. I had a moment two weeks ago when I forgot my Linear set was due Friday at 2pm instead of midnight and I almost missed it. That was scary for like ten minutes. I now check Linear submission time on Thursday nights just in case.

I: What about your tutoring job?

P5: That's on a different schedule. Tutoring shifts are in a Google Calendar my boss runs. I get a text reminder an hour before. That works fine. I don't need to track it myself.

I: Do you ever worry your system will fail?

P5: Sometimes. Especially when other people talk about their planners and apps and color codes. I wonder if I'm getting away with something. But it's been four quarters and nothing major has gone wrong, so.

I: One change to Camino?

P5: Make the PDFs load faster. That's it. I don't need calendar features.
